How Our Technology Works

Our face detection system employs a multi-stage approach:

1
Image Preprocessing

Images are converted to grayscale and enhanced using histogram equalization for optimal feature detection

2
Feature Detection

Haar-like features are used to identify facial patterns at multiple scales and positions

3
Cascade Classification

A cascade of classifiers progressively filters out non-face regions with increasing complexity

4
Result Optimization

Detected faces are analyzed for confidence scores and optimized for accuracy
